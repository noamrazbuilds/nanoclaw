## ENTRY 1: ICOR Framework

summary: ICOR stands for Input, Control, Output, Refine — a four-stage, tool-agnostic productivity framework developed by Paperless Movement. It is designed to work with any combination of software tools a user already has, without requiring a rebuild of existing systems. The framework maps onto four domains: PKM, PPM, BKM, and BPM (personal and business knowledge/project management).
key_claims:
- "The productivity framework that works with every tool you already use — no rebuilding, no starting over."
- The four stages (Input, Control, Output, Refine) cover the full lifecycle of information and tasks.
- ICOR applies equally to personal and business productivity contexts.

  organizations: [Paperless Movement S.L.]
  products: [ICOR Framework, ICOR Framework Builder, myICOR platform]
  tags: [productivity, PKM, PPM, framework, tool-agnostic]
sources:
- url: https://myicor.com/features/icor-framework/
  date: current (evergreen page)
  credibility: high
  confidence: high
  confidence_reason: Primary official source, directly accessible and internally consistent.
conflicts: none

The ICOR framework is the intellectual and commercial core of the entire myICOR platform. Each letter maps to a phase: **Input** is the centralized entry point for all incoming information; **Control** is where inputs are processed into notes, tasks, or references; **Output** organizes items into four functional quadrants; **Refine** covers regular reviews and weekly resets to keep the system alive. The framework is explicitly tool-agnostic — the founders position this as a key differentiator, arguing that most competing systems are built around a specific app (e.g. Notion templates, Obsidian vaults) and become obsolete when users switch tools. ICOR, by design, can absorb any tool change without a system rebuild.

The four domains ICOR covers: Personal Knowledge Management (PKM), Personal Project Management (PPM), Business Knowledge Management (BKM), and Business Process Management (BPM). This breadth — spanning individual and organizational scales — is central to how the founders pitch ICOR to both individual professionals and enterprise clients.
